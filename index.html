About Us

Thusong Funeral Service is a dedicated mortuary based in the rural Thaba-Tseka district. We provide professional and compassionate funeral services to families during their most difficult times.

Our Services

  • Embalming & preparation of the deceased
  • Funeral arrangements & memorial services
  • Holding funeral services
  • Burial arrangements
  • Catering services for memorials and funerals

Products We Offer

  • High-quality caskets and coffins
  • Burial vaults
  • Memorial goods & accessories

Why Choose Us?

Death is a universal human experience. Our services address the urgent need for professional funeral care in a region with limited access. Our local presence ensures that families in Thaba-Tseka receive the support they need.
